Right-click this link and add it to your Favorites:
SPCOM

Go to any sharepoint site and click the bookmark to get the toolbar to pop up. To close it, just click the bookmark again.

The toolbar will grab any pages on the site that are in a Document Library and add them under Nav.

If you click on the arrow next to the name of a List, Document Library, or Survey it will take you to its default view.
Or you can click the name and it will give you more information. You can click the name of the List on this pop up and go to the list settings.
